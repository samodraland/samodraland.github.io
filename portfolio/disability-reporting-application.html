Disability Reporting Application

Portfolio

Disability Reporting Application is a digital platform created to support social agencies in managing disability-related cases more effectively. It provides a centralized solution where users can submit reports about individuals with disabilities, their needs, and the type of assistance required. This reporting feature empowers both field officers and community members to easily communicate cases, ensuring that no individual is left unnoticed.

At the core of the system is a structured data management process. Every report submitted is stored in a secure and organized manner, allowing social agencies to monitor and track disability cases with greater accuracy. The system also enables staff to review the type of support provided — whether it’s medical equipment, accessibility tools, financial aid, or rehabilitation programs — and keep a record of the progress over time.

Language: Javascript, PHP
Frontend Framework: Vue 2, Quasar Framework
Backend Framework: Snappy
Mobile: Cordova
Database: MySQL
